WELCOME TO UNIVERSITY OF KUBMAKONAM

University of Kumbakonam Educational and Charitable Trust was found in the year 1919 July 15 with the noble aim of providing quality education to poorest of poor in our society and having virtuous objective of brining educational institutions in various fields. We welcome all the career concerned potential youth to the dynamic & vibrant academic world of UNIVERSITY OF KUMBAKONAM to experience the environment that emphasis on interaction, innovation & interdisciplinary approach to education. The opportunities are wide open to those interested in pursuing quality education in an academically & attitudes as well as aesthetically ambient premises.
